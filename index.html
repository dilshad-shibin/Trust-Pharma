History About Trust

Trust Pharmaceuticals, (Estd. 1995) is a Pharmaceutical Distribution company based out of Kochi (Kerala, India)

We are a licensed wholesale distributor of prescription / branded and generic medicines as well as dental healthcare medicines, animal/pet health medicines, etc.
